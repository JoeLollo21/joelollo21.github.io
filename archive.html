The Vibe Archive

This is my personal collection of media that I have been "vibing" to at work and home, since the start of this website's current iteration. As it is now, I update it monthly.

I did this as a way of documenting things that I am incredibly fond of - music, gaming, and movies - and how my taste in them influences my life.



October 2024 (current vibe): Dookie: The 2024 Remaster by Green Day and BRAIN

Why?: I discovered this on Instagram and found it to be perhaps THE most creative way to interpret existing music. Essentially, Green Day collaborated with DJ BRAIN to release their beloved album Dookie on a range of old technology - from floppy disks to a Game Boy to a talking fish toy - to show how music is timeless even as technology involves. Such a cool message and interpretation!


September 2024: "Sonic & Chill" mixed by Low Poly

Why?: I grew up on the Dreamcast Sonic the Hedgehog games, namely the Sonic Adventure series, and finding this mix on one of my first days of work helped me feel calm and excited at the same time when I was transitioning to a new job. I'd often listen while doing my onboarding or working on introductory projects.
