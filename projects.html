Hello!

I am a graduate student in the Master of Library and Information Science (MLIS) program at the University of Washington Information School, as well as a graduate assistant specializing in instructional design for the LibID team at the UW Libraries.

My academic and career interests include instructional design, digital humanities scholarship and pedagogy, and data literacy. I am particularly interested in gamification across academic disciplines, and the relationships between game-based concepts and data and information literacy.

I previously earned my BA in English Literature and Media Arts from the School of Interdisciplinary Arts and Sciences at the University of Washington Bothell.
