Baloney Detectives

A Virtual Misinformation Escape Room

by Andrew CharltonJones, Joe Lollo, Luka Taing, and Sabrina Hsu

Statement of Work

Misinformation is a pressing global issue -- thanks to the Internet and various social networks on it, is increasingly easy to post false or misleading information on the internet and reach massive audiences. Inspired by Carl Sagan's famous essay and phrase, Baloney Detectives is an online escape room made to mimic the psychological and emotional responses elicited by real-life interactions with digital misinformation.

In a battle of the facts against the health influencer Vercingetorix, this escape room aims to equip players with the necessary skills to identify misinformation online and become skeptical digital citizens.

This project was made for LIS 547 at the University of Washington, taught by Dr. David Hendry, in Winter 2023. We later presented it, on Professor Hendry's recommendation, at MisInfo Day 2023.
