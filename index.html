Hi! I'm Joe Lollo.

I am a graduate student in the Master of Library and Information Science [MLIS] program and Certificate in Digital Humanities at the University of Washington in Seattle. I also work for the UW Libraries as a Student Specialist for Instructional Design Services.

I am an aspiring Instructional Designer interested in higher education, E-learning, and open-source content creation. I believe in the power held by digital technologies to facilitate educational innovation, and it is my personal mission to advocate for adult learners across multimodal academic environments and provide equitable access to learning.

For more information, please see my CV and index of projects, and feel free to contact me with either of the links to your left.
