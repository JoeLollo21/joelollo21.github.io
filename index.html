Hi, I'm Joe Lollo!

I'm an Instructional Designer based in Seattle, WA, passionate about the intersection of technology and design with equitable education.

I'm currently a graduate student in the MLIS (Master of Library and Information Science) program at the University of Washington's Information School, and work as a graduate assistant for the UW Libraries' Instructional Design (LibID) team.

I feel a deep responsibility to reach out to students and provide them with training, programs, and services that assist them in reaching their goals and expanding their horizons. I use a variety of design methods and technologies to ensure an accessible and engaging educational experience.

If any of that sounds great to you, you can connect with me through email, at lollo21[at]uw[dot]edu.
