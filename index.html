Hello, I'm Joe Lollo!

/low-low/ [like the apple bottom jeans song]

I am a librarian, creative technologist, and "full-time nerd." currently working as a Research & Instruction Librarian at Seattle University. I received my Master's in Library & Information Science from the University of Washington in 2024.

I am passionate about demystifying research practices, creating inclusive experiences, and showing college students that their learning is valuable in the world. You can poke around at examples of my work here on this site.

Some of my non-work interests include: music, movies, reading, gaming, exploring the PNW with friends, and 2000s technology & aesthetics.

Please feel free to reach out if you want to collaborate or simply think one of my projects is neat! My email is joelollo880 [at] gmail [dot] com.
