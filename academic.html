UW Libraries Storytelling Fellows: (2023-present)

As a graduate assistant, I helped revamp the UW Libraries' Storytelling Fellows program, a multi-day online workshop focusing on content creation for digital humanities projects, into an asynchronous format.

Storytelling Fellows is an asynchronous workshop surrounding audio production and academic podcasting, with the goal of giving undergraduate and graduate students in the humanities more transferable skills. As a part of the team, I recorded video lectures teaching the fundamentals of the digital audio workstation (DAW) Audacity, and transferred the original content from Canvas to Omeka, a more streamlined content management system.
